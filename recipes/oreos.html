Chocolate Dipped Oreos

Description

These quick chocolate dipped oreos with peppermint are the easiest gift you can make for your friends and family. Dip tasty oreos into silky smooth semi-sweet chocolate and sprinkle with crushed peppermint for a chocolate peppermint crunchy cookie treat. Plus they're oreos so they'll definitely be a crowd pleaser.

Ingredients

  • Oreos
  • Chocolate chips
  • Peppermint candy

Steps

  1. Pour 12 oz chocolate chips into microwave safe bowl, microwave for about 2 minutes, taking bowl out every 45 seconds to stir. Continue to cook and stir until chocolate chips are melted and silky. Set aside.
  2. Line 2 baking sheets with parchment paper or reusable baking mats and pour 1 cup of crushed peppermint candy into shallow bowl. Dip each Oreo cookie halfway into chocolate, then place on baking pan.
  3. Once all cookies are on the baking sheets, sprinkle peppermint candies liberally over cookies.
  4. Refrigerate until chocolate hardens, at least 15 minutes. Once hardened, you can distribute them into plastic bags or jars as gifts. Or refrigerate until you eat them.
