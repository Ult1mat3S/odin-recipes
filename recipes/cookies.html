Chocolate chip cookies recipe

Description

You'll be sure to love this amazing and easy 5-Ingredient Chocolate Chip Cookie recipe! I mean c'mon, who doesn't like cookies?

Ingredients

  • 3 cups all-purpose flour
  • Â¾ cup brown sugar
  • 2 eggs
  • 1 cup butter, softened
  • 1 cup semi-sweet chocolate chips

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C).
  2. Sift flour and brown sugar into a bowl; stir in eggs. Mix butter into flour mixture until dough is creamy; fold in chocolate chips. Spoon dough onto a baking sheet, keeping them about 3 inches apart.
  3. Bake in the preheated oven until slightly brown, 10 to 15 minutes. Cool.
